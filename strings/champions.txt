</div>		<h1 class="firstHeading"><span><span>1987 IAAF World Indoor Championships</span></span></h1>
		<div id="bodyContent">
			<h3 id="siteSub">From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia</h3>
			<div id="contentSub"></div>
									<div id="jump-to-nav">Jump to: <a href="#column-one">navigation</a>, <a href="#searchInput">search</a></div>			<!-- start content -->
			<p>The <b>1st IAAF World Indoor Championships in Athletics</b> were held in <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Indianapolis" class="mw-redirect" title="Indianapolis">Indianapolis</a>, <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/United_States" title="United States">United States</a> from <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/March_6" title="March 6">March 6</a> to <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/March_8" title="March 8">March 8</a>, <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/1987" title="1987">1987</a>. The championship had previously been known as the <b>World Indoor Games</b>, which were held once before changing the name.</p>
<p>Being the second championship of its kind, there were a lot of
championship records. In fact, new championship records were set for
every single women event. There were a total number of 419
participating athletes from 85 countries.</p>
